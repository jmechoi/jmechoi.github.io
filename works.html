Embrace Ministry Summer Retreat Booklet

Front & Back Cover

I had a lot of fun designing the cover for this retreat booklet. The retreat's theme was "True Righteousness". I decided on a dark background with a big, handwritten font for the title that would easily bring the eyes immediately to it (with an emphasis on "True"). I was really happy with how the schedule turned out on the back cover and wanted to make it as easy as possible for the audience to know when and where to be during the retreat. I used vibrant colors and cute, rounded boxes to catch the eye and bring a little fun into something that could be really boring to look at.

Intro Pages

All inside pages were printed in black and white. The first page reintroduces the theme of the retreat and reveals the theme Bible verse. I chose to keep the design very simple and clean - no distractions to the focus (which is the content).

Inside Pages

These are a sample of the inside pages that made up the rest of the booklet. These booklets would get a lot of use for writing notes throughout the retreat. I designed this booklet to have a lot of open space so that the audience would be able to have plenty of room to write, and in however way they want. I used my own past experiences of feeling constrained with wide-ruled note pages and left the main note-taking pages very open and almost blank. A lot of times it's hard to hear and/or remember the passages that the speaker speaks on - so I also added a small box that clearly states the passage. I also paid attention to the type of paper and made sure that it feels nice to write on, and would be smudge free.
